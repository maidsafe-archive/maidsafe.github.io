Community

For an ownerless and freed Internet to survive and grow, there must be a strong base of community support. The SAFE Network already has attracted hundreds of people looking to participate from the start and expect this interest to accelerate further everyday.

Here are some of the ways you can get involved...

The central point for our community is at MaidSafe.org. Here you can find discussions on community projects and find various levels of support.

Find people and events in your area related to MaidSafe and Project SAFE. Or start a group yourself!
