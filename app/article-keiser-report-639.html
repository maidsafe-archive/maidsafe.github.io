Episode 639 - Interview with David Irvine and Nick Lambert

Keiser Report
12 August 2014

Max Keiser and Stacy Herbert discuss peer-to-peer and decentralized systems, an idea whose time has come, thus, making them stronger than all the armies in the world. In the second half, Max interviews Nick Lambert and David Irvine of MaidSafe about their new company and currency, SafeCoin.
