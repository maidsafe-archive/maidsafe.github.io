MaidSafe Announces New Project to Decentralize the Internet

Coin Report
8 April 2014

MaidSafe has just announced at Inside Bitcoins NY its new project of revolutionizing the internet to become more safe and secure to use. The goal is to make the internet decentralized, independent and anonymous similar to the working of the digital currency known as bitcoin.

The project has utilized $5 million and has been in the works for about eight years now. In order to fund this new internet advancement, MaidSafe is introducing its own digital currency called safecoin.
